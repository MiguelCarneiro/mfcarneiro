I finished my degree in computer engineering after doing an intership at SONAE Sierra, where I was requested to develop a server application in a ASP.NET MVC project, for collecting and consulting the attendance records of employees housed in shopping centers managed by the company. The application had to communicate with all RFID terminals installed in each shopping centre, collect the information and store it in a database developed in SQL. It also allowed users to consult their records in the form of a list or through charts created by Google Charts.

The project was evaluated by ISEP with a final grade of 15 (out of 20).
