About Me


Hello!

My name is Miguel Carneiro and I was born and raised in Oporto, Portugal.

When I turned eight years old, my parents gave me the best gift that I could ask for - my first computer, a ZX Spectrum. Since then, I've been charmed and fascinated by the field of computers science.

Since 2001 until today, I've been working in several major companies, such as CPCIS, Unicer, NOS Systems and SONAE Sierra, as a computer technician. During this time, I've acquired experience and knowledge in computer networks, SAP systems, AD management, videoconferencing systems and providing support to company's software and all kind of computers.

In 2017 I finished my degree in Computer Engineering at ISEP and I look forward to a career change, acquiring knowledge and good practices in the field of development.
